The Innsbruck boulder and lead world cups are the premier and (imo) most exciting events on the world cup circuit. The women's and men's lead final will take place on Friday night, 25th June, Innsbruck time. So come join us at 31 Harcourt Pde Rosebery on Saturday, 26th June, 1pm for a lead world cup viewing party. Bring your favourite movie snacks/plates and drinks and we will provide the rest. We will have our own friendly games/competitions with a climbing wall, hangboard, possibly a slackline and more. So bring your game face and climbing shoes if you wish to participate. And, please avoid social media on the day so you don't ruin the results for yourself and others!
